Spot Purchasing

Quality cold-stored T90 pellets available when you need them

Spot Purchasing

All of our hops are stored as T90 pellets in 22lb and 44lb packages. We can break these down into smaller sizes with a repacking fee, including volume purchases of 1oz and 4oz home-brewer sizes for resale. Minimum order size is 11lbs per variety.
